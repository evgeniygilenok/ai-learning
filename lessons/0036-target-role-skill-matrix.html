Lesson 0036 - 35 minutes

Turn job descriptions into a proof plan.

Before adding more features, translate the market into evidence. A skill matrix keeps the roadmap pointed at jobs instead of vague self-improvement.

The One Idea

A job description is not a wish list to memorize. It is a set of claims you need to prove. For each recurring requirement, decide what artifact in your portfolio will make that claim believable.

Requirement

What the market repeatedly asks for: RAG, evals, agents, observability, security, product sense, or deployment.

Evidence

A repo artifact, demo, trace, eval report, design doc, or interview story that proves you have done the work.

Gap

The missing proof. Be honest: "read about it" is weaker than "built and measured it."

Next Action

One concrete portfolio change, outreach message, or interview drill that closes the gap.

Build Assignment

Create docs/target-role.md in your portfolio repo.

  • Pick 2-3 target titles from `plan-v4.md`.
  • Collect 10 job descriptions or summaries.
  • Extract 8 recurring requirements.
  • Map each requirement to portfolio evidence.
  • Mark each row as strong, partial, weak, or missing.

Ask the agent to review the matrix and identify the highest-leverage proof gap.
