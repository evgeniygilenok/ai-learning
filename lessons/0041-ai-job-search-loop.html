Lesson 0041 - 45 minutes

Treat the AI job search as a feedback system.

Applications, outreach, interviews, and portfolio updates should form one loop. The loop starts before you feel perfectly ready.

The One Idea

The job search is not separate from the learning plan. It is how you test whether your evidence matches the market and whether your explanations land with real people.

Market scan

Save roles, extract requirements, and update the skill matrix every week.

Human conversations

Talk to engineers, hiring managers, founders, ex-colleagues, and community members.

Targeted applications

Apply where your proof package maps to the role. Avoid spraying roles that want research depth you are not claiming.

Interview drills

Practice project walkthroughs, AI system design, RAG debugging, agent safety, and senior behavioral stories.

Weekly Loop

5 saved roles
3 human conversations
3-5 targeted applications after Stage 4
8-12 targeted applications after Stage 6
1 interview drill
1 portfolio update from feedback

Positioning Sentence

Senior TypeScript/product engineer moving into applied AI systems: RAG, evals, agents, observability, security, and production workflows.

Use this as the spine for your resume summary, LinkedIn headline, outreach, and portfolio intro. Then back it with evidence.

Interactive Drill: Strong Application Signal?

"I built a RAG assistant with 30 golden questions and a before/after retrieval report."

"I am passionate about AI and have used ChatGPT daily."

"I can walk through a failed trace and explain why the eval gate blocked the release."

Build Assignment

Create a job-search tracker next to your skill matrix.

  • Track role, company, title, requirements, fit, evidence, contact, status, and next step.
  • Write 3 outreach templates: ex-colleague, AI engineer, hiring manager/founder.
  • Prepare a 10-minute portfolio walkthrough.
  • Prepare one RAG debugging story and one agent safety story.
  • After each conversation or interview, update one portfolio artifact.

Ask the agent to mock interview you using one saved job description and your portfolio README.
